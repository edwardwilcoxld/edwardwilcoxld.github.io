About Me

I’m a GCSE student with a passion for theatrical lighting design. As Co-Lighting Designer at my school in Oxfordshire, I create lighting for productions such as 'Joseph and the Amazing Technicolor Dreamcoat', 'The Addams Family', and the upcoming 'Legally Blonde'. I specialise in board programming, rigging, and creating effects using the Zero88 FLX S48 and ETC Ion XE 20 consoles.


Outside of school, I volunteer at my local community theatre, working alongside experienced technicians and designers. This lets me develop my skills in programming, rigging, and show operation, all while adapting to the fast-paced world of live performance.


Music has always been a big part of my life. I play the drums and dabble in piano, and this background gives me a natural sense of rhythm and timing. It’s especially valuable when programming cues for musicals and synchronising lights with music.


I also train new members of my school lighting team, teaching them how to confidently run live shows from initial ideas to polished performances. Sharing my knowledge is something I really enjoy, and I also learn fresh techniques and ideas from the team.


I’m dedicated to developing my craft as a lighting designer. With experience in both school and community theatre, and a strong musical background, my goal is to study lighting design further and build a career in the industry.
